About Wild Jasmine

Discover our passion for natural beauty, sustainable practices, and the art of handcrafted skincare

Our Story

Born from Passion

Wild Jasmine began in 2018 when founder Sarah discovered the transformative power of natural ingredients after struggling with sensitive skin for years. What started as a personal journey in her kitchen quickly blossomed into a mission to create pure, effective skincare for everyone.

Every product is carefully crafted in small batches using time-honored methods and the finest natural ingredients. We believe that what you put on your skin should be as pure and nourishing as what you put in your body.

Commitment to Quality

From sourcing the finest organic botanicals to hand-pouring each candle, quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our products are free from harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, and unnecessary additives.

We work directly with sustainable farms and ethical suppliers who share our values of environmental responsibility and fair trade practices. This ensures that every purchase supports not just your wellbeing, but the wellbeing of our planet and communities.
